How You Log Your Progress

HOW IT WORKS

When you start, you get two custom Excel sheets on WhatsApp — one for training (with form-check videos baked into every exercise) and one for nutrition + body tracking. Here’s the weekly loop.

01

Every Morning

Weigh yourself empty-stomach, post-bathroom, no clothes. Log it in the sheet. Open the Stepz app, log yesterday’s steps. Track your meals against the macros.

02

In The Gym

Open today’s training sheet. Tap any exercise to watch the coach’s form-check video. For every set log weight × reps × RIR (reps in reserve, 0–3). Cells auto-colour: 🟢 progressed · ⚪ maintained · 🔴 regressed.

03

Every Sunday

Take your waist measurement — dry, not flexed. Rate your week 0–10: sleep, hunger, stress, recovery, digestion, training energy, carb tolerance. Average your weekly steps.

04

Coach Rebuilds Your Plan

Send both sheets back to the coach on WhatsApp. He reads every row, looks at the colour pattern, your self-rating and your waist trend — then ships your updated plan for the week ahead. Repeat.

One stagnant week? No change — the coach respects your body’s normal fluctuations. Two stagnant weeks? Food or effort gets adjusted. The decision rules come from real numbers in your sheet, not vibes.

Common Questions

THE STUFF EVERYONE ASKS BEFORE STARTING

I’ve failed with coaches before. Why would this be different?

Most coaches send the same PDF to every client. CMYH plans are written from scratch for one person and rebuilt every Monday based on what your last week actually looked like. If your real life changes, your plan changes.

Can online coaching really work without seeing me in the gym?

Yes — over 500 documented client transformations say so. You send form check videos via WhatsApp; coach reviews and replies same day. The accountability is often stronger than in-person because everything is logged.

What if my schedule is chaotic — shift work, travel, ramadan?

That’s exactly when custom coaching pays off. Plans get rebuilt around shift patterns, time zones and ramadan fasting windows. We've coached pilots, doctors, gulf-based engineers and ramadan-cutters.

How long until I see real results?

Visible changes by week 4 if you follow the plan. Measurable body composition shift by week 8. The 12 to 16 week mark is where most transformation photos come from.

Do I need supplements?

Almost never required. If anything is recommended (e.g. whey, creatine, vitamin D), it’s targeted to your goal — never a stack of branded powders pushed for commission.

What does it cost?

Depends on program duration and which package you pick. Message on WhatsApp with your goal and timeline — you’ll get a clear quote within the day.
